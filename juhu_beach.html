About Juhu Beach

Juhu Beach is one of the most famous beaches in Mumbai, India. It is known for its wide expanse of sandy shores and vibrant atmosphere. The beach is a popular spot for both locals and tourists, offering a variety of activities such as street food, horse rides, and beautiful sunsets.

Activities at Juhu Beach

  • Enjoy local street food like pav bhaji and bhel puri
  • Take a horse ride along the shore
  • Watch the stunning sunset over the Arabian Sea
  • Participate in beach sports like volleyball and cricket

Attractions near Juhu Beach

  • Visit the iconic ISKCON Temple
  • Explore the nearby Versova Beach
  • Shop at the lively Juhu Market
  • Take a stroll along the Bandstand Promenade
